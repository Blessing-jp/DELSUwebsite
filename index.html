Vice Chancellor’s Welcome Message!

My Dear New Students

It is my great pleasure to welcome you to Delta State University, Abraka, as you embark on this exciting journey of higher education. We are delighted to have you join our prestigious university and I trust that you will find your time here worthily enriching and fulfilling. We are committed to providing you with an outstanding environment where you can thrive academically, socially and personally. I encourage you to make the most of your time here and enjoy all we have to offer.

It is pertinent I offer you a few words of advice here:

Be cautious of scammers, especially those who operate through various phantom WhatsApp groups or as touts around campus. These individuals and sometimes even syndicates may try to mislead you with false information, feigning assistance or offer illegitimate services. To avoid falling victim, always take instructions only from your official Departmental, Faculty and University channels. Remember, all fees should be paid through the University’s designated portal and approved banks. Once again, welcome to our glorious university and I wish you unadulterated success in your academic pursuits.
